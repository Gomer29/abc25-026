Repair Services

At GLY Motor Group, we understand the importance of keeping your vintage muscle car in top condition. Our expert mechanics specialize in repairing and restoring classic cars to their former glory. Whether you need routine maintenance, engine repairs, or a complete restoration, our team is here to help.

Our repair services include:

  • Engine diagnostics and repairs
  • Transmission repairs
  • Brake system repairs
  • Suspension repairs
  • Electrical system repairs
  • Bodywork and paint restoration
  • Interior restoration
  • Custom modifications and upgrades
  • Oil changes and lubrication

We use high-quality parts and the latest techniques to ensure that your vintage muscle car receives the best care possible. Contact us today to schedule a repair service or to learn more about how we can help you preserve your automotive treasure.
